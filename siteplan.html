Site Plan: AutoSmart Hub

Osun AutoSmart Hub

AutoSmart Hub

This name reflects a digital hub for car owners and enthusiasts looking to stay informed about modern smart car technologies and vehicle maintenance tips. It’s easy to remember, brandable, and communicates the theme clearly.

Optional Domain: autosmarthub.tech

Site Purpose

The purpose of AutoSmart Hub is to educate and guide car owners on modern automobile technologies—such as electric vehicles, autonomous features, and infotainment systems—while also offering practical car maintenance tips and model comparisons. The site will include:

  • Trends in smart vehicle technology
  • Maintenance guides and scheduling tips
  • Comparison of popular vehicle models and features
  • A form for asking maintenance-related questions or requesting consultation

Scenarios

  • What regular maintenance does my electric vehicle need compared to a traditional car?
  • Which SUV offers the best combination of smart features and fuel efficiency?

Color Schema

The following color palette was chosen to communicate professionalism, clarity, and modernity.

#003366
Navy Blue
#f57c00
Orange Accent
  • Navy Blue: Used for headings, logo text, and primary backgrounds.
  • Orange: Used for buttons, form highlights, and accent text.

Typography

  • Primary Font: Segoe UI (for headings and body text)
  • Backup Fonts: sans-serif

This modern sans-serif font is highly readable across devices and aligns with the technical feel of the content.
